Oily Skin

Oily skin results from hyperactive sebaceous glands, causing excess sebum production. It appears glossy with enlarged pores and is prone to blackheads, pimples, and blemishes. While this sheen may be appealing, it can lead to skin issues. However, oily skin often ages slower due to its natural moisture.
