Normal Skin

Normal skin is balanced, with good hydration and a healthy barrier. It has a smooth, even texture, small pores, and retains moisture well, giving it a soft, supple feel. People with normal skin rarely experience breakouts or sensitivity but may occasionally face dryness or blemishes due to environmental or hormonal changes.
